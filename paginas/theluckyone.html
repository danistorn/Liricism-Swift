"The lucky one" was chosen by Taylor to be track number 13 for being this her lucky number (she was born in December 13, 1989). She gave the number a special protagonism through all her career drawing it on her hand before shows in many of her tours, releasing albums on days 13 or dates that sum up 13, etc.

"The lucky one" has an introduction of 13 seconds and she mentions the word "lucky" 13 times.
